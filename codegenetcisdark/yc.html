What is Competitive Programming ?

Competitive programming is solving well-defined problems by writing computer programs under specified limits.

"People very often mistake programming competitions for the measure of being a great programmer in general. The truth is that neither one implies the other." - By Mimino


Why competitive programming

Competitive programming trains your mind to think faster and to think in new ways that can solve problems very efficiently.
Itâ€™s like when you are an ex-footballer, you can easily enter the domain of basketball if you want to, because you already have the muscular mass and the agility needed to perform these kind of sports and the only thing you need to focus on is what are the rules of basketball, how to use you hands instead of you legs, how a achieve some goals,etc.
Thus, competitive programming help you build a solid base of computer science knowledge that will give you great benefits in the future when you want to learn anything simpler or relatively easier.
When you go out in the real world, you just are not supposed to solve problems the way they are in competitive coding.

In brief competitive coding benefits you in below mentioned ways:

  1. Competition keeps you alive in the real world.
  2. It increases and update your knowledge and will develop your skills.
  3. Competitive programming is fun and needs focus at the same time.
  4. It will help you in your job interview.
  5. You will became an extremely good programmer.
