Vision And Mission

We at CodeGenetics strives to inculcate the approach of Problem Solving in students.The teaching pattern and video contents at CodeGenetics is not only limited to solving questions but to grasp how to think about a problem.

We provide learning of Data Structure and Algorithm to the students aspiring to excel as a Software Engineer.We prepare students for ACM-ICPC,Google Code Jam ,Facebook Hackercup and other competitions and along with it we prepare students for Jobs at tech gaint companies such as Google,Amazon,Walmart.
